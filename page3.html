Garlic Bread

Ingredients:

1 loaf French stick

50g butter

2 cloves garlic (crushed)

Method:

1. Cut breadstick into slices, stopping before fully cutting through.

2. Mix crushed garlic and soft butter together.

3. Spread garlic butter onto each side of cut breadstick slices.

4. Wrap breadstick in foil and heat in a slow oven for 20 minutes.

Garden Salad

Ingredients:

1 bunch Romane lettuce leaves

1 punnet Cherry tomatoes

1 Lebanese cucumber

1 Red onion

2 tbs Italian salad dressing

Method:

1. Wash all vegetables.

2. Cut cherry tomatoes in half. Cut cucumber and onion into thin slices.

3. Mix lettuce leaves, tomatoes, cucumber and onion in a large salad bowl.

4. Add salad dressing to salad when ready to serve.

Caesar Salad

Ingredients:

1 whole Cos lettuce

1/2 French breadstick (broken into small pieces)

3 eggs (boiled)

1/4 cup Parmesan cheese

1/2 cup cooked bacon (cut into small pieces)

1/3 cup Ranch dressing

Method:

1. Chop lettuce into large pieces.

2. Mix lettuce, broken breadstick, cheese and bacon bits into a large salad bowl.

3. Mix Ranch dressing into salad.

4. Chop boiled eggs into large pieces and add to salad. Keep chilled until ready to serve.

Hawaiian Pizza

Ingredients:

1 large pizza base

2 tbs Pizza Sauce

1/3 cup chopped pineapple

1/2 cup bacon bits

1/2 cup Mozzarella cheese

Method:

1. Preheat oven to moderate.

2. Spread pizza sauce onto pizza base.

3. Sprinkle cheese over pizza base. Add bacon onto pizza base.

4. Spread pineapple chunks onto pizza base.

5. Cook pizza in moderate oven for 20 minutes, or until cheese is golden.

Bolognese

Ingredients:

2 cups spiral pasta

1 tin chopped tomatoes

3/4 cup tomato sauce

1/4 cup HP sauce

500g mince

1 brown onion (diced)

Method:

1. Brown mince and onion in hot saucepan.

2. Turn saucepan to low heat. Add chopped tomatoes, tomato sauce and HP sauce.

3. Leave to simmer for 30 minutes.

4. Boil pasta until al dente.

5. Add cooked pasta to sauce. Serve hot with Parmesan cheese.

Carbonara

Ingredients:

2 cups Penne pasta

3/4 cup cooking cream

1 brown onion

1/2 cup bacon bits

Method:

1. Boil pasta until al dente.

2. Put boiled pasta into clean saucepan. Add cooking cream and simmer on low.

3. Dice onion and cook until tender.

4. Add onion and bacon bits to pasta. Simmer for 15 minutes.

5. Serve hot with Parmesan cheese.

Mushroom Risotto

Ingredients:

1 1/2 cups Aborio rice

1L vegetable stock

2 cups chopped button mushrooms

Method:

1. Saute mushrooms until soft.

2. Put rice, stock and cooked mushroom into deep oven dish.

3. Cook in moderate oven for 1 hour, or until rice is tender.

4. Serve hot.

Chicken Parmigiana

Ingredients:

2 chicken breasts

2 tbs Tomato Sauce

2 bacon rashers

1/2 cup Mozzarella cheese

Method:

1. Preheat oven to moderate.

2. Spread tomato sauce onto chicken.

3. Place a bacon rasher on each chicken breast.

4. Sprinkle cheese onto each chicken breast.

5. Cook chicken in oven for 30 minutes, or until chicken is well cooked.

6. Serve hot with roasted or steamed vegetables.

Chocolate Souffle

Ingredients:

4 tbs sugar

60g butter (soft)

3 tbs plain flour

3/4 cup milk

200g dark chocolate

2 eggs (seperated)

1/2 cup cream

1 tsp vanilla essence

Method:

1. Preheat oven to moderate.

2. Grease souffle dishes with canola oil.

3. Melt butter in saucepan. Add flour and stir until smooth.

4. Remove mixture from heat and slowly add milk while stirring.

5. Return mixture to medium heat and stir until mix boils and thickens.

6. Stir in chocolate and sugar. Remove from heat, add egg yolks.

7. Whisk egg whites until firm peaks. Fold 1/3 of egg whites into chocolate mixture

8. Put mixture into greased dishes and bake for 20 minutes.

9. Once cooked, dust souffles with icing sugar. Serve hot.
